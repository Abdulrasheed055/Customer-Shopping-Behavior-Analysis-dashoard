# 🛒 Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

Understanding customers is no longer optional—it’s a competitive advantage.  

This project leverages transactional data to uncover trends, optimize marketing, and guide product strategies for a retail company.

---

## 🎯 Project Aim
To answer the key business question:  
*"How can consumer shopping data be leveraged to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing & product strategies?"*

---

## 🔍 Process (Step-by-Step)

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Cleaned and standardized **3,900+ customer records** across demographics, purchases, and behavior.  
- Engineered new features: `age_group` and `purchase_frequency_days`.  
- Resolved missing and inconsistent categorical values (e.g., `review_rating`, `promo_code_used`).  
- Loaded cleaned data into PostgreSQL for structured SQL analysis.

### 2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Analyzed behavior across **4 age groups** and **4 product categories**.  
- Evaluated impact of **reviews, discounts, subscriptions, and shipping types**.  
- Identified repeat-purchase patterns and purchase frequency trends.  
- Generated actionable insights using **Python (Pandas, NumPy)** and SQL.

### 3️⃣ Visualization & Dashboarding (Power BI)
- Built an interactive **Power BI dashboard** with key KPIs and visuals.  
- Implemented slicers for **Gender, Category, Subscription Status, and Shipping Type**.  
- Designed executive-level metrics for quick business decision-making.

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- **Total Customers:** 3,900+  
- **Average Purchase Value:** $59.76  
- **Revenue by Gender:** $157,890 male vs $75,101 female  
- **Revenue by Category:** Clothing $104K (top), Accessories $74K, Footwear $36K, Outerwear $19K  
- **Revenue by Age Group:** Young Adults $62K (highest), followed by Middle-aged, Adults, Seniors  
- **Subscription Adoption:** 7.3% subscribed, 92.7% non-subscribed  
- **Average Customer Rating:** 3.75 (moderate satisfaction)

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
1. Increase **subscription adoption** to potentially improve customer lifetime value by 20–30%.  
2. Bundle **Clothing + Accessories** to raise average order value.  
3. Target marketing campaigns toward **Young Adults**, the most active buyers.  
4. Reevaluate **Outerwear** for pricing or seasonal optimization.  
5. Improve customer experience to raise ratings above 4.0+, supporting retention and loyalty.

---
